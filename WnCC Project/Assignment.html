Introduction

Bonjour! I am Geetanksha Gupta, pursuing M.Sc PhD dual degree program in Energy Science and Engineering from IIT Bombay. Currently I am doing a summer course on Web development organised by Web and Coding Club (WnCC), IIT Bombay.

Apart from the academics, I will tell about my other interests.

I like travelling to new places. Being from jaipur, obssessed with forts but yeah, beaches can be much more fun!. Want to do new adventures in life.

“Music is the literature of the heart; it commences where speech ends.” ― Alphonse de Lamartine. Music is the best healing therapy for me as well.

The only real stumbling block is fear of failure. In cooking you’ve got to have a what-the-hell attitude. — Julia Child. Now, cooking is the new emerging passion of mine.
